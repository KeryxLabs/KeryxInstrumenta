Ingredients

1 cucumber, diced

2 tomatoes, chopped

¼ onion, very thinly sliced

Salt

Optional: splash of lemon juice or vinegar

Steps

Dice cucumber and tomatoes into similar-sized pieces.

Thinly slice onion.

Toss everything in a bowl with salt (and lemon/vinegar if available).

Let sit while shrimp cooks — flavors deepen naturally.

👉 Teaching moment: osmosis + flavor absorption.

Ingredients

Shrimp (peeled & deveined)

½ onion, sliced

1–2 cloves garlic (if available)

Salt & pepper

Oil or butter

Steps

Heat pan with oil (medium heat).

Add onions first — cook until slightly soft.

Add shrimp in a single layer.

Cook 1–2 minutes per side until pink and opaque.

Season lightly.

👉 Teaching moment: protein denaturation (why shrimp changes color).

🎨 Plating Idea
Shrimp on one side (warm, golden tones)

Fresh salad on the other (cool reds & greens)

Optional: serve over rice if available
